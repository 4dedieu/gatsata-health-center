Advice for Clients:

Stay Consistent: Consistency is key to seeing results with physiotherapy. Make sure to attend all your scheduled sessions and follow your therapist's instructions for exercises at home.
Communicate Openly: Don't hesitate to communicate any concerns or changes in your condition to your physiotherapist. They are there to support you and can adjust your treatment plan accordingly.
Patience is Important: Recovery takes time, so be patient with yourself. Trust the process and celebrate small victories along the way.
Focus on Prevention: Even after you've achieved your goals, continue with maintenance exercises to prevent future injuries and maintain your progress.
Take Ownership of Your Health: Remember that you are an active participant in your own healing journey. Take responsibility for your health by following your therapist's recommendations and making healthy lifestyle choices.
